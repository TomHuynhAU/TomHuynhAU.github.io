Frequently Asked Questions

What is Liberty City RP?

It's a unique project based on GTA V, allowing thousands of players to interact with each other in real-time.

What is Role Play?

IIt's a game mode where participants create characters and scenarios, and then act according to their roles within the game processes.

How to start playing Liberty City RP?

First, you need to download the licensed GTA V and install RAGE MP Launcher.

What is RAGEMP Launcher?

Is a multiplayer modification for Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) that allows players to create and join custom multiplayer servers.

What to do on the server?

In addition to a wide range of professions and interactions with players, we have regular updates and seasonal events available.
